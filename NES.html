Nintendo Entertainment System



The Nintendo Entertainment System, also know as NES for short, was released in July of 1983 in Japan but it was called Famicom which is short for Family Computer. When it first came out in america in 1985 the retail price in the store was $299. The system came with 2 controllers and the system. There was also a deluxe version of the console that came with a Data Recorder, Keyboard, Joystick, Zapper Light Gun, and 2 controllers and the System itself.
