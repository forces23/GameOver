Sega Genesis



On August 14th of 1989, Sega Enterprise released the Sega Genesis in North America for the price of $189. There #1 selling game for the sega was the famous Sonic the HedgeHog with 15 million copies sold. When they mad the sega they also made it possible to play the old Sega Master System Games using a converter that you would insert into where the normal 16-bit Genesis games would go. They also made two additional add ons that would allow you to play the sega Saturn Games and the other add on was the 32x addon which made it possible to play the 32 bit game cartridges on the Sega Genesis. Between the years 1989 and 1999 Sega Enterprise sold over 30 million Segas around the world!
