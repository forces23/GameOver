Atari 7800



The Atari 7800 AKA the Atari 7800 ProSystem came out 9 years after the Atari 2600 in May of 1986. It was designed to be backward compatible with almost all the Atari 2600 games. When the system first came out it sold for an affordable $140, which was far less then the Atari 2600. The Atari 7800 had way better graphics compared to the 2600 and came with a different type of joystick then the 2600 came with.
