Colecovision



Colecovision came out at the end of the summer of 1982 in August, and sold for $175. The game system was a closer feel to playing games in the arcade, which was an all time favorite for kids in the 80's. When they built the colecovision they had the idea of the players being able to buy extensions for the console to pretty much mod the system. There were 2 extension modules you could purchase, Expansion Module #1 let the players play any of the Atari 2600 games on the colecovision. Expansion Module #2 was a steering wheel expansion where the players could use an actual steering wheel when playing the racing games.
