Nintendo 64



The Nintendo 64 came out in september of 1996 in North America and you purchase it for $199.99. When they came up with the N64 they where trying to compete with the modern game systems of the time, like the Playstation and the Sega Saturn. Instead of switching to CDs they decided to stick with the cartridges they felt like it was easier to pirate CDs then to pirate the cartridges. When it was first released they only had 2 games that went out with it and that was Super Mario 64 Pilowings 64.
