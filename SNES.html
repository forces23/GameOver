Super Nintendo Entertainment System



Selling for the price of $199, on August 23rd of 1991 Nintendo released the Super Nintendo Entertainment System to North America. Compared to the Nintendo Entertainment System the SNES had way better graphics and way better sound quality then any other consoles at the time that it was released. Out of all the games that Nintendo mad for the SNES the #1 selling game was Super Mario World with over 20 million were copies sold.
