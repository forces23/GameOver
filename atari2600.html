Atari 2600



The Atari 2600 was first released to the public on september 11 of 1977. When the computer gaming console first was sold on the shelves, it came with 2 joysticks and 2 paddle controllers and came with a game cartridge called combat that had 10 games on it with 27 variants to make each time you play it different. They were charging between $199-$229 which was a good chunk of change back then and even today. The atari company only expected a 2-3 year selling life, they didn't expect it to still be sold even today and highly looked for by video game collectors or even people wanting to remeber their childhood.
